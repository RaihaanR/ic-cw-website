Our aim

Charity Week is an amazing annual campaign to raise money for orphans and needy children across the globe. In association with Islamic Relief, CW is a week dedicated to bringing smiles to many children in every continent, and this is your chance to be a part of that goal! The objective is simple: raise as much money and awareness for orphans and needy children around the world so that they receive the shelter, education and care that they deserve. All whilst having fun yourself!
